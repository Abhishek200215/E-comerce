Tracking FAQ

Why isn't my tracking number working?

Tracking numbers may take 24-48 hours to activate in the carrier's system. If it's been longer, please contact us.

My package shows delivered but I didn't receive it

Check with neighbors, building management, or safe delivery spots. Contact us within 48 hours if not found.

Can I change the delivery address after shipping?

Address changes are difficult once shipped. Contact us immediately, and we'll attempt to reroute with the carrier.

Delivery Issues & Support

Lost Packages

If your tracking shows no movement for more than 5 business days, contact us immediately.

  • We'll initiate a trace with the carrier
  • If lost, we'll reship or refund
  • 48-hour investigation period

Damaged Items

If your items arrive damaged, please contact us within 7 days of delivery.

  • Photos of damage required
  • We'll send replacements immediately
  • Return shipping provided

Delivery Delays

For packages significantly past their estimated delivery date.

  • Check tracking for updates first
  • Contact us for assistance
  • We'll work with carrier for resolution
