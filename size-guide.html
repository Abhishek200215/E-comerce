Bra Size Guide

Step 1: Band Size

Measure snugly around your rib cage, directly under your bust.

Step 2: Bust Size

Measure around the fullest part of your bust.

Step 3: Calculate Cup Size

Subtract band size from bust size to find cup size.

How to Measure

What You'll Need

  • Soft measuring tape
  • Well-fitting undergarments
  • Full-length mirror
  • Assistant (recommended)

Bust/Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your bust/chest, keeping the tape level and snug but not tight.

Waist

Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist, usually about 1-2 inches above your navel.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 7-8 inches below your waist.

Inseam

Measure from the crotch seam to the bottom of the ankle along the inside of your leg.
