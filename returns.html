Return Eligibility

Eligible for Return

  • Items in original condition
  • With original tags attached
  • Unworn and undamaged
  • In original packaging
  • Within 30 days of delivery

Not Eligible for Return

  • Final sale items
  • Worn or damaged items
  • Items without original tags
  • Personalized/custom items
  • Underwear & swimwear*

Special Notes

Defective Items

If you receive a defective item, contact us within 7 days of delivery for an immediate replacement or refund.

Wrong Items Received

If you receive the wrong item, we'll provide a prepaid return label and expedite the correct item to you.

Return Process

1

Initiate Return

Log into your account and go to "My Orders" to start the return process, or contact our customer service team.

  • Provide reason for return
  • Select return method
  • Print return label (if applicable)
2

Prepare Package

Carefully package your items following these guidelines:

  • Include original tags
  • Use original packaging if possible
  • Include return form
  • Securely seal the package
3

Ship Return

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location:

  • USPS locations
  • UPS stores
  • FedEx offices
  • Keep your tracking number
4

Receive Refund

Once we receive and process your return:

  • Refund processed within 5 business days
  • Notification email sent
  • Refund appears in 3-10 business days

Policy Exceptions

Final Sale Items

No Returns

These items are marked as final sale and cannot be returned:

  • Clearance items
  • Special promotion items
  • Items marked "Final Sale"

Intimate Apparel

Limited Returns

For health and safety reasons, these items have restrictions:

  • Underwear (unworn with hygiene seal)
  • Swimwear (with protective liner intact)
  • Hosiery (unopened packaging)

Custom Items

No Returns

Personalized or custom-made items cannot be returned:

  • Monogrammed clothing
  • Custom-sized items
  • Made-to-order pieces

International Returns

Special Conditions

Additional considerations for international returns:

  • Return shipping not covered
  • Customs duties not refundable
  • Longer processing times

Returns FAQ

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. It may take an additional 3-10 business days for the refund to appear on your account, depending on your bank or credit card company.

What if I received a damaged or incorrect item?

Contact us immediately at returns@fashionfusion.com or call +1 (555) 123-RETURN. We'll arrange for a prepaid return label and expedite the correct item to you at no additional cost.

Can I return an item I purchased on sale?

Yes, sale items can be returned within 30 days unless marked "Final Sale." Sale items are refunded at the purchase price paid.
